Design Overview

A fully modeled medical suction pump designed for hospital ward mobility. This project involved creating individual components (canisters, motor housing, frame, casters) and mating them into a functional assembly.

The design focuses on stability, ease of canister access, and ergonomic mobility.

Components Modeled

  • Main Body: Sheet metal housing

Modeling Approach

The tube body was created using a revolved profile to ensure consistent wall thickness. A centerline was used as the axis of revolution, with inner and outer profiles fully constrained to define a uniform 2 mm wall thickness.

Features include tangent arcs for the curved base and a modeled lip for the gripping interface, utilizing fillets to reduce stress concentrations.

Key Specifications

  • Outer Diameter: 17 mm
  • Length: 80 mm
  • Wall Thickness: 2 mm
  • Material: Polypropylene Copolymer (PP-co)
Material Selection Rationale:

PP-co selected for high chemical resistance and durability in lab environments.

Parametric Modeling & Design for Manufacturability (DFM)

Parametric Centrifuge Tube

Injection Molding Design
SolidWorks Parametric Modeling DFM

Modeling Approach

The tube body was created using a revolved profile to ensure consistent wall thickness and rotational symmetry. A centerline was used as the axis of revolution, with inner and outer profiles fully constrained to define a uniform 2 mm wall thickness.

The curved base was generated using tangent arcs to replicate standard geometry. The top lip was modeled as a separate feature with a gripping interface, utilizing fillets to reduce stress concentrations.

Key Specifications

  • Outer Diameter: 17 mm
  • Inner Diameter: 15 mm
  • Total Length: 80 mm
  • Wall Thickness: 2 mm
  • Material: Polypropylene Copolymer (PP-co)
Material Selection Rationale:

PP-co was selected for its high chemical resistance, durability, and standard application in laboratory consumables.

About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering undergraduate at the University of Ghana with strong interests in machine learning, medical imaging, biomedical device design, and applied healthcare technologies.

My academic and project experience spans cancer image segmentation, CAD-based medical device design, telemedicine systems, and applied biomedical research. I enjoy translating engineering and computational ideas into practical solutions for real-world healthcare challenges.

I am currently undertaking my National Service at the Accra Psychiatric Hospital, where I am gaining exposure to clinical environments, healthcare systems, and biomedical equipment in a mental health setting. This experience is shaping my interest in technology-driven solutions for mental health care and clinical decision support.

My long-term goal is to contribute to innovative research and development in biomedical engineering, particularly at the intersection of machine learning, medical imaging, and healthcare systems.

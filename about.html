About Me

Who I Am

I am a Biomedical Engineering graduate from the University of Ghana with a passion for bridging the gap between clinical medicine and modern technology. My work sits at the intersection of Machine Learning, Medical Imaging, and Device Design.

I enjoy translating complex engineering and computational ideas into practical solutions for real-world healthcare challenges. My academic background has provided me with a strong foundation in cancer image segmentation and telemedicine systems.

My long-term goal is to contribute to innovative research and development in biomedical engineering, specifically focusing on how AI can assist in clinical decision support.
